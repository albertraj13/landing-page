Jiraiya the Legendary Sanin

Jiraiya is a ninja from the village of Konohagakure trained by Hiruzen Sarutobi, the Third Hokage.He is also known as the "Toad Sage"

Pupil's of Jiraiya

Naruto Uzumaki, the pupil that took on the will of Jiraiya sensei

Minato Namikaze, who later became the Fourth Hokage and Naruto's father.

Nagato Uzumaki, he later became the leader of the Akatsuki.

Konan, Jiraiya trained Konan, along with Nagato and Yahikoi.
